About us

Sugar is an online shopping assistant that lives in your browser and instantly turns you into a smarter shopper. Sugar automatically presents pricing information on any product on Amazon and allows you to buy at the right time.
How it works
Whenever you shop online, Sugar kicks in, goes to its database in and automatically extracts a price graph history on the product you watch. Is it the right time to buy? Sugar will auto-apply a coupon if it finds one. Still hesitating? No worries, add the product to your watch list, and Sugar will let you know when the price drops. Sugar is available on Chrome, Firefox, & Edge.
